Welcome to June Plum Restaurant!

June Plum is a little home away from home in the heart of Croydon, London. With fresh, authentic Jamaican ingredients we promise that the flavours of Jamaica will burst in your mouth from the very first bite. Our drinks are traditional and refreshing and in the evening, we open our little wine bar section for customers to listen to soothing sounds from all over the Caribbean, including music from Barbados, Grenada and Jamaica of course. Here at June Plum, we will give you a memorable experience. Check out our contact page to find us and browse our online menu to make your mouth water in anticipation.
